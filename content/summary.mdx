## Key Strategic Frameworks

### 1. The Three Pillars
Every feature is rated across three dimensions:
- **Data Dependence**: Does the client have the data?
- **Governance Required**: Can they maintain it?
- **Operational Safety**: Can they break it?

### 2. Investment Levels
Not every feature needs the same polish:
- **MVP**: Make it work (Low cost)
- **Standard**: Solid foundation (Medium)
- **Polished**: Self-service ready (High)
- **Premium**: White-glove (Max)

### 3. Data Remediation Paths
When data is missing:
- **Pay Now**: Fix data first (Better outcomes)
- **Pay Later**: Launch with gaps (Faster)
- **AI Enrichment**: Generate missing data (Modern approach)
